ABOUT US

As we all know, there are fewer and fewer wild animals in the world and the rare wild animals protected by the local are at risk of being dying. Many of us, as part of the Earth's ecological composition, human, are not aware of the magnitude of the problem.

Animal World's Organization was established in 1999 with the main tasks of collecting data on all kinds of animals around the world, filing documentaries about the real life of wildlife and Contributing to the protection of animals.

It was established by some of international volunteers who put protecting animals in account and consider that it is one of the most essential duties in their lives.

Since its establishment, we have got much support and sponsorships from animal protectors as well as many fans. In order to let more people know about animals, wildlife and get more close to nature, our company decided to establish a news website with the theme of animal, and update information and articles about animals world. The purpose is very clear, try our best to make more and more people understand animal world and recognize rare wildlife. Actually, only after understanding these organism, human learn to protect them and the earth will last for long, and human beings and animals will live longer as well.
